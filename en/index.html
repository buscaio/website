Busca.io

In some countries, like Cuba, not everyone can connect to the internet. Yes, we are still waiting. Busca.io helps people inside that country search for products/ jobs/services using their email accounts (most of them have email accounts, but no internet connection). We do not generate any content, we just search. If you are reading this from your phone, connected to your home Wifi, the good news is that you do not need this service and you are, in some small and essential way, a lucky person. However, If you would like to start using this service or you know someone that could use it, send an email to ayuda@busca.io . You will receive an email with instructions.

1: Send an email to one of our email addresses.
In the subject of the email
specify what you are looking for (separated by commas).

2: Wait a few seconds

3: Receive in your inbox an email with the results

Available email addresses

We decided to start with one of the most popular websites : revolico.com (Cuban Craigslist version). Check here: email addresses

How it works?

We allow upt to 4 search terms by email sent:
Example: You want to search on the section Buy / Search

Send an email to: busca.compraventa@busca.io
Email Subject: IPhone 6, Ford Focus parts, Microwave, AC
Result: Our system searchs on the website revolico.com section Buy/Sell for each search term independently: "IPhone 6", "Ford Focus Parts", "Mircorwave", "AC", and then sends an email with the results.
